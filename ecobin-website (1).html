The Problem

India's Waste Crisis
is Escalating

160,000 metric tonnes of municipal solid waste is generated daily in India — yet less than 30% is properly processed. The result: overflowing landfills, soil contamination, and lost recycling potential.

  • 🗑
    No Awareness: Most citizens don't know how to segregate wet, dry, and hazardous waste correctly.
  • 📍
    No Convenience: Finding recycling centres is difficult with no tracking or scheduling tools.
  • 💡
    No Incentive: Zero reward or recognition for eco-friendly behaviour creates no motivation.
  • 📊
    No Visibility: Municipalities have zero real-time data on waste collection efficiency.

How It Works

Four Steps to a Greener Future

Our seamless process makes sustainable waste disposal effortless and rewarding.

01

Scan the QR Bin

Open the EcoBin app, scan the QR code on any smart bin in your area to begin your recycling session.

02

AI Waste Detection

Our AI camera module identifies waste type — organic, plastic, metal, e-waste — and guides proper disposal in real time.

03

Earn Reward Points

Every correct disposal earns EcoPoints. Redeem for discounts, coupons, or donate to environmental NGOs.

04

Track Your Impact

See your personal sustainability score, CO₂ savings, and leaderboard rank. Share achievements with friends.
